TransitAnalystIsrael

TransitScore Israel
The relative intensity of the transit service in Israel by location. Scores are normalized to 0-100. Displayed as interactive map. Click on heatmap to get score for location. Useful for comparing transit service level between locations / neighborhoods within a city or town.

Municipal TransitScore
The relative average intensity of the transit service in cities and towns in Israel. Scores are normalized to 0-100. Displayed as interactive map. Click on municipality to get score. Useful for comparing transit service level between cities / towns.

Municipal FairShareScore
The relative investment in transit service in cities and towns in Israel. Scores are relative to the average national investment per person. E.g. FairShareScore = 100% investment similar to national average, FairShareScore = 150% investment 50% higher than national average, FairShareScore = 70% investment 30% lower than national average. Displayed as interactive map. Click on municipality to get score. Useful for comparing investment in transit service between cities / towns.

Municipal Transit Ranking
Municipal ranking by TransitScore, FairShareScore and BuiltDensityScore displayed as charts and lists. Search and sort lists by city / town name or score. Use slider to zoom in on different areas of the chart. Click on name or chart to highlight city / town. Useful for comparing scores between cities / towns.

Municipal trips per day (tpd) per line
Display the trips per day (tpd) for each transit line that serves a selected city or town. Displayed as interactive map. Click on municipality to get list of lines and tpd sorted by line frequency. Useful for analyzing in detail the transit service in a city / town and for recommending improvements.

Frequent Service Lines
Frequent service lines displayed on an interactive map. Use slider to set level of service to display from 60+ trips per day (tpd) to 240+ tpd. Click on map shows list of frequent lines in the area sorted by frequency. Useful for analyzing what neighborhoods or cities or towns are served by high frequency service and which are not.

Buses per day on street
A list of all the lines and their frequency on the street segment selected on a map. The list is sorted by frequency and includes the total number of buses that pass through the selected street segment in a day. Useful to analyze the potential benefit of a dedicated bus lane on a street.

Accessible level of service at stops
Level of transit service accessible within walking distance from any location. Displays the total number of transit opportunities per day at all stops. Click on a stop to get a breakdown of all the lines at that stop sorted by their frequency. Useful for analyzing what areas in a city or town do not have access to a reasonable level of service and what needs to be improved.

Accessible level of service to and at train stations
The level of service on feeder buses near train stations and the level of service of trains at stations. Also show level of transit service at any point of interest (PoI) and the level of service from this PoI to train stations. Useful for analyzing the balance of feeder buses per train at train stations. Also useful for analyzing the accessibility by transit of train service to neighborhoods and other locations of interest like job centers, hospitals, universities, etc…

TransitTimeMap
Accessible area for a given duration of travel from/to a location by transit (or bike or walking). Useful for analyzing access by transit to jobs, education, health services, train stops, municipal services, etc… Also useful for comparing levels of accessibility for different neighborhoods in a town or city.
